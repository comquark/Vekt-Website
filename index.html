Vekt for iOS and watchOS

The fastest weight tracker.

Vekt is a fast and simple weight tracker for iOS and watchOS. It has never been so delightful to track your weight and form a healthy habit.

Features

Health Integration

Track your weight simply fast and get it all into iOS’s Health app.

Reminders

Enable reminders to get notified and form the healthy habit of weight tracking.
You can even enter a new weight measurement right from the notification!

Automatic BMI Calculation

Body mass index offers a bit of insight on how healthy your weight actually is.

Targets

Set a target weight so you’ll get more motivation to track it.

Other Features

Siri Shortcuts

Setup a shortcut for automations and voice control.

Custom Units

Support for Pound, Kilo and Stone.

Line Chart

Subtle chart for progress indication.

Dark & Light Themes

Different themes and native iOS 13 theming is coming this Fall.

Height Entry

Adds your height into the health app.

Interactive Notifications

Enter your weight directly from the notification.

Motivational Messages

Optional random motivation messages.

Siri Suggestions

Get the suggestion from Siri in spotlight.
